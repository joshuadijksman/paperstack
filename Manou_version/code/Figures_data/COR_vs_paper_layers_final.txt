COR as a function of the number of paper layers for different paper surface densities.
    These are the averaged experimental data used for the main comparison between paper types.
    The error values are the standard error of the mean where repeated measurements were available.

Datasets in this file:
- 40 g/m2 paper: Final averaged COR data for 40 g/m2 paper.
- 80 g/m2 paper: Final averaged COR data for 80 g/m2 paper, new paper.
- 120 g/m2 paper: Final averaged COR data for 120 g/m2 paper.
- 160 g/m2 paper: Final averaged COR data for 160 g/m2 paper.
- 250 g/m2 paper: Final averaged COR data for 250 g/m2 paper.

Laser stack-height measurements as a function of the number of paper layers.
    The measured stack height is compared to the paper-only estimate, showing the contribution of air gaps between sheets.

Datasets in this file:
- 80 g/m2 measured stack height: Laser displacement measurement of total stack height for 80 g/m2 paper.
- 80 g/m2 paper-only estimate: Estimated paper thickness without air gaps, using single-sheet paper thickness.
- 120 g/m2 measured stack height: Laser displacement measurement of total stack height for 120 g/m2 paper.
- 120 g/m2 paper-only estimate: Estimated paper thickness without air gaps, using single-sheet paper thickness.

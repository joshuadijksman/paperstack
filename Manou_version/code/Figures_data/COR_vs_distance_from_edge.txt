COR as a function of distance from the edge of the paper stack.
    This checks whether edge effects influence the measured coefficient of restitution.

Datasets in this file:
- 5 paper layers: COR measured at different distances from the edge for a stack of 5 paper layers.
- 10 paper layers: COR measured at different distances from the edge for a stack of 10 paper layers. No repeated-measurement SEM included.
- 50 paper layers: Retry measurement for a stack of 50 paper layers.
- 100 paper layers: COR measured at different distances from the edge for a stack of 100 paper layers.
- 300 paper layers: COR measured at different distances from the edge for a stack of 300 paper layers.
